About Airasia Airline

AirAsia Berhad is a Malaysian low-cost airline headquartered near Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. In terms of fleet size and destinations, it is the largest airline in Malaysia. AirAsia Group regularly operates domestic and international flights to more than 165 destinations in 25 countries/regions. Its main hub is klia2, which is the low-cost airline terminal at Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA) in Sepang, Selangor, Malaysia. Its subsidiaries Thailand AirAsia, Indonesia AirAsia, Philippines AirAsia and India AirAsia have established locations at Bangkok-Mumbai, Jakarta-Sukarno-Hatta, Manila-Ninoy Aquino and Bangalore-Kempegorda Airports. There is a hub, and its sister airline AirAsia X focuses on long-distance routes. AirAsia’s registered office is located in Petaling Jaya, Selangor, and its headquarters is located at Kuala Lumpur International Airport.
